Introduction & Background

I've worked as a photographer, reporter, editor and various other jobs in journalism and government. For the past 11 years or more, web development has been my growing passion. From 2005 to 2015, I worked as a multimedia specialist for the Wyoming Department of Workforce Services' Research & Planning section, building static websites and editing publications and online content. Not content with producing static websites the same way every day, I began to learn new web technologies and methods.
